Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Overview

Your privacy matters. Mod Mail is designed with a strict no-data-collection policy. This document explains what information the bot accesses and how it is handled.

2. Data Collection

Mod Mail does not collect, store, or log any user data. The bot does not maintain databases, analytics, or any form of persistent storage for messages, user IDs, server information, or any other data.

3. Message Handling

Messages sent to the bot are forwarded directly to the designated staff channel in your Discord server. The bot acts as a relay and does not retain copies of any messages after delivery.

4. Third-Party Services

Mod Mail does not share any information with third-party services. The bot communicates solely through the Discord API to facilitate message forwarding.

5. Discord Data

The bot requires certain Discord permissions to function (such as reading and sending messages). These permissions are used only for the bot's core functionality and nothing else. The bot accesses only the information necessary to route messages between users and staff.

6. Children's Privacy

Mod Mail does not knowingly collect information from anyone. As the bot stores no data, there is no special handling required for minors beyond Discord's own Terms of Service.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the contact form on the main site.
