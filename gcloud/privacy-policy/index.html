Privacy Policy

Audit Importer processes information provided by users within the Google Spreadsheet where the tool is installed.

Information processed may include:

  • Audit data pasted by users.
  • Spreadsheet content required to generate audit reports.
  • Basic Google Apps Script execution data provided by Google.

Audit Importer does not sell, share, transfer, or disclose user data to third parties.

Audit Importer does not store data outside of the Google Spreadsheet where it is used and does not transmit audit data to external servers.

Information received from Google APIs is used solely to provide the functionality of the application and is handled in accordance with the Google API Services User Data Policy.
